Command GenFormPhIon

Comand format

GenFormPhIon

This command computes the dipole operator formulas and energy experssions for photoionization

Data records used

  • OrbOcc, orbital group occupations of the target state.
  • ScatContSym, IR of the continuum orbital.
  • ScatSym, IR of the total scattering state.
  • SpinDeg, spin degeracy of the total scattering state.
  • TargSym, IR of the target state.
  • TargSpinDeg, the spin degeneracy of the target state.
  • InitSym, IR of the initial state.
  • InitSpinDeg, the spin degeneracy of the initial state.
  • OrbOccInit, orbital group occupations of the initial state.

Data records created

  • EngForm, contains the expression for the interaction potential.
  • DirProdOvlp, contains the matrix that transforms the direct product function into the symmetrized functions.
  • DipOpForm, contains the information needed to construct the correct dipole matrix elements.
